ABOUT US

Established over 10 years ago, we're a small cafe'/coffee shop hidden off the main street of beautiful, historic Ybor City, FL (just outside Downtown Tampa) serving you coffees - made with locally roasted Buddy Brew beans, teas - steeped to order just for you, host to music & art shows, and much more.


What we stand for:

  • Great quality at a great price: At Coffee Cup, we select our ingredients very carefully to
    ensure consistency and high-quality products at an affordable price
    our consumers; hygiene and safety are also a top priority.
  • Innovative Products: We offer a wide variety of food and beverage products customized
    to suit our patrons’ needs and preferences. Our product development team is
    continuously innovating and creating new tailored product lines.
  • Great Service: We have long history and experience in staff training along with serving
    different customer segments in a variety of sectors and formats, put us ahead of our
    competition in providing a memorable and satisfying customer experience.
  • Customer Focused: Our customer is King! Everything
    we do and stand for focuses on serving
    our customers and exceeding their expectations.
  • Convenience: We work hard to expand our reach and
    have locations “around every corner” to remain the neighborhood coffee shop of choice.
