WELCOME TO COFFEE CUP SHOP!


Established over 10 years ago, we're a small cafe'/coffee shop hidden off the main street of beautiful, historic Ybor City, FL (just outside Downtown Tampa) serving you coffees - made with locally roasted Buddy Brew beans, teas - steeped to order just for you, host to music & art shows, and much more.
While you're here, be sure to check out our live Facebook feed for current information on our community events, mouthwatering coffee specials, scrumptious food specials, our delightful made-with-love baked goods, and any other pertinent info!


What we stand for:

  • Great quality at a great price: At Cilantro, we select our ingredients very carefully to ensure consistency and
    high-quality products at an affordable price to our consumers; hygiene and safety are also a top priority.
  • Innovative Products: We offer a wide variety of food and beverage products customized to suit our patrons’
    needs and preferences. Our product development team is continuously innovating and creating new tailored product lines.
  • Great Service: Cilantro’s long history and experience in staff training along with serving different customer
    segments in a variety of sectors and formats, put us ahead of our competition in providing a memorable and satisfying customer experience.
  • Customer Focused: Our customer is King! Everything
    we do and stand for focuses on serving our customers and exceeding their expectations.
  • Convenience: We work hard to expand our reach and
    have locations “around every corner” to remain the neighborhood coffee shop of choice.
